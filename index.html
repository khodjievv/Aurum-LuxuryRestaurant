Our Provenance

A Legacy of Restraint & Obsession

Aurum was conceived as a single question: what does it feel like to eat without compromise?

Chef Elara Voss trained eleven years across three-starred kitchens in Paris, Kyoto, and Copenhagen before arriving in Tashkent with one mandate — nothing enters this dining room that is not extraordinary. Every ingredient is sourced directly. Wagyu arrives from a single farm in Kagoshima. Caviar is harvested on demand.
